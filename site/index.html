How It Works

Build a 40-card deck led by a commander. Play minions on a hex-grid board, cast spells, claim tiles for mana, and defeat the enemy commander to win. Every commander has unique abilities, stats, and a patron faction that determines which cards you can play.

Factions

Six patron gods, each with their own playstyle and card pool.

Skaal
Goddess of War

Strong, aggressive minions and destructive magic. Overwhelm your opponent before they can stabilize.

Grenalia
Goddess of Nature

Mana growth, poisons, and massive creatures. Ramp up your resources and crush with overwhelming force.

Lucia
Goddess of Light

Unified armies, healing, and village control. Build a resilient force that outlasts the competition.

Shadis
God of Shadows

Stealth, deception, and dark magic. Strike from the shadows and control the battlefield through cunning.

Archaeon
God of Knowledge

Ancient power, artifacts, and strategic depth. Outthink your opponent with superior knowledge and planning.

Neutral
Unaligned

Mercenaries and creatures available to all commanders. Versatile tools that fit into any strategy.

Starter Decks

Three pre-built decks to get you into the action right away.

Skaal
Starter Goblins

A fast-paced, aggressive deck built around goblin minions. Captain Greenbeard gives Haste to minions played in enemy territory. Overwhelm and evaporate.

Grenalia
Starter Growth

A defensive, mana-focused deck. Lubela shields your minions on arrival. Ramp up your territory, then drop massive threats to close out the game.

Lucia
Starter Healing

A synergy-based deck revolving around healing. Milo heals a character every other turn. Play threats that grow stronger from your healing to become unstoppable.
