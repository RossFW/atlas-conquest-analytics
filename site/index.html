Build Your Deck

Choose a commander and assemble 40 cards from their faction. Each commander has unique abilities that shape your strategy.

Claim Territory

Move your commander across the hex grid to claim tiles. Each tile generates mana to fuel your army.

Defeat the Enemy

Play minions, cast spells, and battle your way to the enemy commander. Reduce their health to zero to win.
